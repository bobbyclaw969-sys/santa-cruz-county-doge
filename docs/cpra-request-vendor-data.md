## REQUEST FOR PUBLIC RECORDS

To Whom It May Concern:

Pursuant to the California Public Records Act (Government Code Section 6250 et seq.), I hereby request copies of the following records:

### Primary Request (Vendor Payments)
1. **All vendor payment records** for Fiscal Years 2023-24, 2024-25, and 2025-26
   - Including: vendor name, payment amount, date, description, department
   - Format requested: CSV or Excel spreadsheet

2. **Complete vendor/contractor database** listing all businesses, nonprofits, and individuals who have received County payments or contracts

3. **All County contracts** (personal services, professional services, construction, grants)
   - Contractor name, contract amount, term, scope of work, department

### Secondary Request (Entities)
4. **Grant award records** - all grants to nonprofits/NGOs receiving County funds

5. **Campaign contribution disclosures** for County officials (Form 700s) - statement of economic interests

### Format
- Electronic format preferred (CSV, JSON, Excel)
- If paper copies required, I will pay duplication fees per County fee schedule
